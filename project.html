What is Self Development ?

Personal development or self-improvement consists of activities that develop a person's capabilities and potential, build human capital, facilitate employability, enhance quality of life, and facilitate the realization of dreams and aspirations.
development may take place over the course of an individual's entire lifespan and is not limited to one stage of a person's life.
It can include official and informal actions for developing others in roles such as teacher, guide, counselor, manager, coach, or mentor, and it is not restricted to self-help.
When personal development takes place in the context of institutions, it refers to the methods, programs, tools, techniques, and assessment systems offered to support positive adult development at the individual level in organizations.

The importance of Self Development

It helps you overcome your weaknesses and work to improve them, as at every stage of your self-development you will turn one of your weaknesses into a strength, so you should not be ashamed of your weaknesses and accept them so that you can overcome them and improve them.

Dale Carnegie

He was an American author, developer of famous lessons in self-improvement, and director of the Carnegie Institute for Human Relations.
He was born in 1888 and died in 1955, after suffering from leukemia of the Hodgkin lymphoma type. he wrote many books Among his books is the book (Stop Worrying and Start Living).

James clear

An American expert in the field of personal development, he provides advice and guidance to major companies in the field of developing work habits and decision-making.He focuses on the importance of simplifying the behaviors and habits that an individual wants to make part of his daily life Among his books is the book (Atomic Habits).
